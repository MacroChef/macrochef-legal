# MacroMeal Support

Need help with MacroMeal? We can help with account access, subscriptions, meal logging, syncing, and general app issues.

## Contact Support

**Email:** macrocheffounder@gmail.com 
**Response time:** Usually within 1–2 business days

If you contact support, include:
- the email tied to your account
- a short description of the issue
- screenshots if relevant
- your device model if the issue is technical

## Common Support Topics

- Sign in and account access
- Subscription and billing issues
- Restore purchases
- Food logging problems
- Syncing issues
- Goal and macro questions
- Bug reports and feature feedback
- Account deletion requests

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I reset my password?
Use the sign-in flow in the app and follow the password reset steps for your account email.

### How do I restore purchases?
Open the premium or subscription area in the app and use the restore purchases option.

### Why is my subscription not showing up?
Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase the subscription. If it still does not appear, contact support.

### Why are my logs not syncing?
This may be caused by a temporary network issue or delayed refresh. Try reopening the app and checking your connection.

### How do I request account deletion?
Email support@macromeal.app from the email associated with your MacroMeal account and request deletion.
