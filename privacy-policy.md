# MacroChef Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

MacroChef respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us.

## Information We Collect

MacroChef may collect information you provide directly, including your email address, profile details, fitness goals, current stats, meal preferences, allergies, saved meals, grocery items, and food logs.

MacroChef may also process photos you choose to upload for food or ingredient scanning. These images are used to provide nutrition estimates or ingredient suggestions.

## How We Use Information

We use your information to provide app features, including meal generation, macro targets, food logging, saved meals, grocery lists, coaching insights, and account management.

We may use third-party services to operate the app, process subscriptions, store data, and provide AI-powered features.

## Third-Party Services

MacroChef may use services such as Supabase, OpenAI, Adapty, and Apple App Store services. These providers may process data as needed to provide authentication, storage, AI generation, subscription management, and payment processing.

## Health And Nutrition Disclaimer

MacroChef provides nutrition estimates and meal suggestions for informational purposes only. MacroChef is not medical advice and should not replace advice from a doctor, registered dietitian, or qualified healthcare professional.

## Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Your Choices

You may update your profile information in the app. You may also contact us to request help with account or data questions.

## Children

MacroChef is not intended for children under 13.
